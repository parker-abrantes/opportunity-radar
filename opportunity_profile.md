# Opportunity Profile

This file describes the ranking profile used by the dashboard. The public demo
uses representative interests and sample opportunities. For a personal version,
replace this with your own target roles, industries, locations, constraints, and
application priorities.

## Example Priority Areas

- Technical program management, project management, operations, and systems roles.
- Nuclear energy, advanced energy systems, aerospace, autonomy, and robotics.
- Paid internships, funded research programs, scholarships, and conference travel grants.
- High-signal opportunities where the work, mission, and application effort justify the time.

## Example Ranking Signals

- Role alignment
- Sector and mission fit
- Eligibility
- Career leverage
- Funding or compensation
- Deadline urgency
- Effort-to-value ratio
